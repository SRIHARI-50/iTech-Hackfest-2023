Who can participate in the Hackathon?

Students pursuing B.E/B.Tech can participate in teams.

Will I get a confirmation for registering in the Hackathon?

Yes, you will be getting a mail from iTech Hackfest Team after the successful registration.

What is the Prize amount for the Winners?

The first prize is Rs. 1.5 lakh, second prize is Rs. 1 lakh, and third prize is Rs. 50 k.

Is this an Online event?

No, it is an offline Hackathon.

What is it in for me?

Learning new skills, looks good on your CV, networking, winning cash prizes, working as a team and meeting industry experts.

What to be done in the Regional round?

The Idea of the project will be presented before the judges for the selection process.

What to be done in the Final round?

The prototype of the project will be presented before the judges for the selection process.

How are teams shortlisted in the Regional Round?

Teams are selected based on their ideation, market product fit, technology

How are the winners selected in the Final Round?

Winners are selected based on their code and design, innovativeness and desirability, usability, demo and presentation.

Is there accommodation provided for the participants in the Final Round?

Yes, Accomadation will be provided by PSG iTech with nominal fee of Rs.500 per participant per day, which includes food also.

Who will be Judging?

Industry veterans from corporate partners and experts from educational partners

Who is keeping the intellectual property of my solution?

The team that develops the project is the owner of the intellectual property and project. We do encourage making the code open source or sharing of code with others in the community, but it is not required to participate in the event. Before the Presentations our technical team will do a quick technical check to make sure that you created your project at the hackathon. You are allowed to use open-source libraries, your own libraries etc. to develop your solution
