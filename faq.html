Who can participate in the Hackathon?

Students pursuing B.E/B.Tech can participate in teams.

Will I get confirmation for registering in the Hackathon?

Yes, you will get a mail after registering successfully.

What is the prize amount for the winners?

The first prize is Rs.1.5 lakh, second prize is Rs.1 lakh, and third prize is Rs.0.5 lakh.

Is this an online event?

No, it is an offline Hackathon.

What is in it for me?

You will have the following benefits by participating in the hackathon: Learning new skills, networking with peers, winning cash prizes, working as a team and meeting industry experts.

What is expected in the evaluation of regional round?

The Idea of the project and the plan for the implementation should be presented.

What is expected in the evaluation of final round?

The prototype of the project should be demonstrated.

How are teams shortlisted in the regional round?

Teams are selected based on their ideation, market product fit and technology.

How are the winners selected in the final round?

Winners are selected based on their (i) code and design, (ii) innovativeness and desirability, (iii) usability, (iv) demo and presentation.

Is accommodation provided for the participants in the regional round?

Please contact the regional SPOC for accomadation and related queries. Click herefor SPOC contact details

Is accommodation provided for the participants in the final round?

Yes, accomadation will be provided at PSG iTech campus. Neelambur, coimbatore with a nominal fee of Rs.450 will be collected per participant per day, including food.

Who will evaluate the projects at Hackathon?

Industry veterans from corporate partners and experts from academic partners will evaluate.

What is the registeration fee for the regional round Hackathon?

The cost for regional round will be Rs.250 per team.

What is the registration fee for the final round?

The cost for final round is Rs.2000 per team.

Is there reimbursement for travel?

There is no reimbursement for travel.

Will there be wireless internet access?

Yes, there will a seperate wireless Internet connection for the participants.

What devices or gadgets should I bring?

Bring your own laptops and electronic gadgets required for coding. Internet facility will be provided to the participants.

Where will the final round take Place?
PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research, Neelambur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.
Do all the team members need to be present in person?

Yes, all the team members must be present in person only.

Who is keeping the intellectual property of my solution?

The team that develops the project is the owner of the intellectual property and project. We do encourage making the code open source or sharing of code with others in the community. You are allowed to use open-source libraries or your own libraries to develop your solution.

What is the cancellation policy?

There is no cancellation and fee once paid will not be refunded.
