Themes

Climate Action

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural, such as through variations in the solar cycle. But since the 1800s, human activities have been the main driver of climate change, primarily due to burning fossil fuels.

Climate Action

Economic Growth and Job Security

Recent turmoils in Technology and Financial industries has led to numerous job loss which has in turn led to loss of mental peace to affected families, financial losses, gloomy mood and general panic in the market.

Guidelines

    Regional Round Hackathon

  • Students pursuing B.E/B.Tech can participate in teams
  • A team can have 2 to 4 members
  • Registration fee for regional round is Rs.250 per team and the last date for registration is 30.06.2023
  • All participants in a team should belong to the same institution
  • During the regional round, teams will be asked to present their idea on one of the given three themes

    • Final Hackathon

  • Selected teams for the final Hackathon must register separately by paying Rs.2000 per team
  • The final Hackathon will take place at PSGiTech, Neelambur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu - 641062
  • In the final Hackathon, teams will be asked to implement and demonstrate the working prototype
