About

iTech Hackfest 2023 is a National Level Hackathon is conducted at PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research for three days during 24.08.2023 – 26.08.2023. This hackathon is organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in association with SAP. SAP Academy of Engineering sponsors the prize money of two lakhs, first prize – Rs.1,50,000, second prize – Rs.1,00,000 and third prize- Rs.50,000. Mr. Ferose V.R., Senior Vice President and Head of SAP Academy for Engineering inaugurated the iTech Hackfest 2023 and delivered the inaugural address. Mr. Ferose explained the importance of perseverance and passion for a successful career and emphasized the advantage of having long-term view. He discussed about sustainability and long term thinking.

Themes

Climate action

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural, such as through variations in the solar cycle. But since the 1800s, human activities have been the main driver of climate change, primarily due to burning fossil fuels.



Economic Growth and Job Security

Recent turmoils in Technology and Financial industries has led to numerous job loss which has in turn led to loss of mental peace to affected families, financial losses, gloomy mood and general panic in the market.



Equal Access to Education and Opportunities

Equal education and opportunities refer to providing all individuals with the same access to education and career opportunities, regardless of their race, gender, socioeconomic status, or any other characteristic.
