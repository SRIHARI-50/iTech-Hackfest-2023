About

iTech Hackfest is a national-level Hackathon, which provides a platform to bring talented engineering graduates from diverse backgrounds together to collaborate and create innovative solutions to real-world problems. This is a fantastic opportunity for participants to showcase their creativity, technical and people skills.

With SAP as the primary sponsor, participants will have the opportunity to interact with industry experts, mentors, and judges who will provide them with valuable insights and feedback on their projects. iTech Hackfest provides an excellent opportunity for participants to network and build lasting relationships with like-minded individuals, while creating innovative solutions. The event is designed to be a fun and engaging experience for all participants, with various activities and challenges.

Themes

Theme 1 - Climate Action

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural such as variations in the solar cycle. But since the 1800s, human activities have been the main driver of climate change, primarily due to burning fossil fuels like coal, oil and gas. Greenhouse gas concentrations are at their highest levels in 2 million years and emissions continue to rise. As a result, the Earth is now about 1.1°C warmer than it was in the late 1800s. The last decade was the warmest on record. The consequences of climate change now include, among others, intense droughts, water scarcity, severe fires, rising sea levels, flooding, melting polar ice, catastrophic storms and declining biodiversity. Given the scenario, Hackathon team invites ideas related to what software solutions can be developed to curb or reduce or slow down this process. Solutions can be in the area of (not limited to) reporting,reducing the consumption, recycling, circular economy and disaster response.



Theme 2 - Economic Growth and Job Security

Recent turmoils in Technology and Financial industries has led to numerous job loss which has in turn led to loss of mental peace to affected families, financial losses, gloomy mood and general panic in the market. The cycles of recessions are happening more quicker than before. Startups and banks are failing at larger rate when compared to what it was earlier. Given the current situation, PSG iTech Hackfest 2023 invites you to propose ideas and software solutions in the areas of: Prediction of next economic downturn, Prediction of early signs of collapse of financial institution, Prediction of over hiring by the companies, Improve Financial status of affected families and Improve mental status of the affected families



Theme 3 - Equal Access to Education and Opportunities

Equal education and opportunities refer to providing all individuals with the same access to education and career opportunities, regardless of their race, gender, socioeconomic status, or any other characteristic. Equal education includes providing equal access to resources such as textbooks, technology, and qualified teachers. Equal opportunities, on the other hand, ensure that all individuals have the same chances to succeed in their chosen career paths. This means eliminating discrimination in hiring, promoting diversity and inclusion in the workplace, and providing equal pay for equal work. Achieving equal education and opportunities is essential for building a fair and just society, promoting economic growth, and advancing human rights. The current state as we know is biased. Given the situation, PSG iTech Hackfest 2023 invites ideas and software solutions that can help provide equal access to education and opportunities. Some examples could be: Most education content on platforms such as Udemy, Coursera or even youtube is available only in English while its missing in many regional language. Many tools lack personalized learning paths based on students abilities. How can we provide access to tools and frameworks in remote parts of the world where internet is not stable?
